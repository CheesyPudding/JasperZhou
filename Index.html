About me

Hello, my name is Jasper, and I am an apsiring technical software/game developer, as well as a creative animator, artist, and designer. Ever since taking programming courses in highschool and self-teaching Adobe Animate, I've continue to gain valuable experience in these various fields and mediums through industry experience, game jams, and personal projects.

Currently, I'm persuing my Bachelors in Computer Science with a specialization in game dev at BCIT. I am also a 2022 Graduate at BCIT's Computer Systems Technology (CST) program. I've also been formally taught 3D Character Animation using Maya at the Vancouver Institute of Media Arts (VanArts).

During my free-time, I do enjoy gaming, working out, working on coding projects, and animating whenever I can. You can find some examples of my projects and works on my Github and Youtube, or you can also find and contact me on Linkedin or by email if you have the time.
