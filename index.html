About me

Hello, my name is Jasper, and I am aspiring to become a technical software/web/game developer, as well as a creative animator, artist, and designer. Ever since taking programming courses since highschool, I've continue to gain valuable experience in these various fields and mediums through industry experience, game jams, and personal projects.

Currently, I'm persuing my Bachelors in Computer Science with a specialization in game dev at BCIT. I am also a 2022 Graduate at BCIT's Computer Systems Technology (CST) program.

During my free-time, I do enjoy gaming, working out, working on coding projects, and animating whenever I can. You can find some examples of my projects and works on my Github and Youtube, or you can also find and contact me on Linkedin or by email if you have the time.
